A pepperoni pizza cooked to perfection

This delicious meal has embeded itself into modern culture and can be found on any corner of the globe. The dish can be found at most big gatherings and is great for any occasion. Being a popular dish across most demigraphics it only makes sense to learn how to make this meal if you find yourself having to feed some mouths.

In this detailed recipe you will be supplied with all of the knowledge needed to create your own homemade pie. Follow the recipe as directed, and you'll hold the spot as host of the year for sure. With that being said, good luck and happy cooking!

Ingredients

Ingredients for the dough

  • 1 1/2 Cups warm water
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  • 2 teaspoos khoser salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

For the pizza/toppings

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Cornmeal (to help slide the pizza onto the pizza stone)
  • tomatoe sauce (smooth or pureed)
  • Firm mozzarella cheese, separated into small clumps
  • Fontina cheese, grated
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Pepperoni, thinly sliced
